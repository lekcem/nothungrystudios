How to Stretch Your Ingredients

Save money and reduce waste by reusing leftover ingredients creatively.

Hover over photos to reveal tips.

Vegetables

Chop and add to soup or chili.

Toss with cold pasta and light dressing for a salad.

Slice and serve with hummus.

Fruits

Add to muffins, breads, or pancakes.

Slice on top of hot or cold cereal.

Freeze to use in smoothies.

Bread

Use for French toast.

Dice for stuffing.

Season and bake for quick croutons.

Chicken

Add to a soup, casserole, or stir-fry.

Use the bones to make stock.

Shred as a salad topping or taco filling.

Eggs

Hard boil for egg salad sandwiches.

Stir-fry with leftover veggies.

Make a frittata.

Beans

Mash as a bean dip with crackers or veggies.

Spread on tortillas with toppings.

Use in salads, chilis, or stews.
